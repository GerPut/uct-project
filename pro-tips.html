PRO TIPS

Use our monthly professional tips at own discretion.

  • ♦ Take it slow. Start out slow train daily but don't push too hard. Instead slowly build on previous days progress. Soon you will have enough confidence for longer runs. - "Andrew Thoms"
  • ♦ Have a plan. Plan your running days weeks ahead and have a back-up if things don't go accordingly. A planner or calender on your fridge will do just fine. - "Susan Bing"
  • ♦ Stay hydrated. Drink your eight glasses of water a day. On training days consume more. Water is important don't skip or replace it with something else.- "Matthew Ross"
  • ♦ Allow recovery. Rest days are just as important as running days. Don't feel like resting too much stay active by going for short strolls on recovery days. - "Pete Wellgo"
  • ♦ Listen to your body. Don't push too hard if something doesn't feel right rather stop. Make sure you get enough sleep every night. - "Bianca Slew"
  • ♦ Try new trails. Challenge yourself is a must to see how you have progressed as a runner. New trails come up everyday. Read reviews, prepare yourself and just do it. - "Mike Tims"
